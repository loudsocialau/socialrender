Privacy Policy

Last updated: 6 June 2026

Social Render respects your privacy. This policy explains how we collect, use and protect information submitted through our website, checkout links and project brief forms.

Information we collect

We may collect your name, email address, company name, website, product details, brand assets, product photos, creative examples, project notes and any other information you submit through our forms or by email.

Payments

Payments are processed through Stripe payment links. Social Render does not store your full card details on our website. Stripe may collect and process payment information in accordance with its own privacy and security practices.

Forms and uploads

Project briefs and file uploads are collected through Tally. Please only upload assets that you own, control, or are authorised to share with us for the purpose of creating your project.

How we use your information

We use your information to process your order, understand your brand and product, create your visual content, communicate with you, deliver files, manage revisions, improve our services and meet legal or administrative requirements.

Sharing your information

We may share information with service providers that help us run the business, including Stripe, Tally, Vercel, email providers, cloud storage providers and creative production tools. We do not sell your personal information.

Storage and security

We take reasonable steps to protect the information you provide. However, no online platform, email system or cloud service can be guaranteed to be completely secure.

International services

Some of the platforms we use may store or process information outside Australia. By submitting information to Social Render, you acknowledge that your information may be handled by these third-party services.

Access, correction and deletion

You can request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal information by emailing us.

Contact

For privacy questions, contact socialrender@outlook.com.
